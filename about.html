Founder Story

Built Out of Frustration.
Perfected by Obsession.

My name is Ruslan. I built TrailVolt because no bag I could find did what I actually needed it to do. This is the story of how that frustration became a system.

The Problem with Every Other Pack

I used to own four different bags. A laptop backpack for work. A weekend duffel for travel. A trail pack for hiking. A gym bag I mostly hated. Four bags. Constant repacking. Forgotten chargers. The wrong shoes. Missing documents.

It wasn't just inconvenient — it was mentally exhausting. Every Sunday evening I'd be reorganising for Monday. Every Friday afternoon I'd be repacking for the weekend.

I tried expensive backpacks. Premium travel bags. The ones written about in gear review blogs with five stars and inflated prose. They all failed me in the same way: designed for one type of journey.

"I wanted one pack that could do everything — not because I was lazy, but because the right system should be that capable."

— Ruslan, Founder

The Idea

The concept came to me on a Thursday morning in Dublin. I was at Dublin Airport — Everyday backpack on my back, wishing it were a travel bag — and I watched someone in front of me pull a whole secondary bag off their main pack. It was modular.

What if the modules weren't just the bag configuration — but the accessories, too? Phone holder, power bank, shoe compartment, and document sleeve all snapping on and off the same base pack?

Why It's Called TrailVolt

The name came from the idea of energy — the voltage that a journey gives you. When you're carrying the right gear, there's a different quality to how you move through the world.

"Trail" because every journey is one. "Volt" because the right system charges you up for it.

How We Build

Our Process

Every TrailVolt product goes through a minimum of six physical prototyping rounds before it ships. We test in real conditions — not labs.

1

Problem-First Design

Every feature starts as a documented user problem. If we can't clearly articulate the frustration it solves, it doesn't get built.

2

Real-World Prototyping

Minimum six physical samples per product. Tested in commutes, airports, hiking trails, and everyday Dublin weather.

3

Premium Materials Only

We source Cordura, ballistic nylon, YKK zippers, and Duraflex hardware — and publish exactly what goes into every product.

4

Lifetime Support

Five-year warranty. Lifetime repair programme. We built TrailVolt to last — and we back that up with actions, not just words.
