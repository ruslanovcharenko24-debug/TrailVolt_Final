The Everyday 22L is ideal for daily commutes, day trips, and short city breaks. The Travel 30L is built for 3–7 day trips and frequent flyers who carry a laptop and clothes. The Trail 28L is optimised for hiking, with load distribution and weatherproofing as priorities. If you're between two sizes, go larger — you can always leave space empty.

Modules use our patented snap-lock connector system. Align the module's mounting plate with the front connector panel of the pack, push until you hear a click, and it's secure. To remove, press the release tab on the bottom of the module and slide up. No tools required. The whole process takes under 3 seconds.
