The One Pack System

TrailVolt Core Pack was built from a simple frustration: why do we own five different bags for five different contexts? The Core Pack is one system that covers all of them — not through compromise, but through intentional modularity.

The base pack is engineered for daily carry: padded 16" laptop compartment, organised front panel, anti-theft back pocket, and a weather-resistant exterior built from recycled high-density nylon. From there, you snap on what the day demands.

Eight modules. One attachment system. No compatibility issues, no adapters, no tools. The Power Bank goes in for a long flight. The Chest Phone Module for a busy city morning. The Rain Cover for the trail. The pack stays the same — your configuration doesn't.

16" Laptop Compartment

Dedicated, padded sleeve with shock-absorbing side walls and a soft-touch inner lining. Compatible with every MacBook Pro and major Windows laptop up to 16 inches. Fleece-lined base prevents scratching.

Modular Attachment System

All eight modules attach via a reinforced magnetic clip system on the standardised front face. Connects in under 3 seconds. No tools, no adapters — remove and reattach across any line.

Weather-Resistant Construction

DWR (Durable Water Repellent) coating on the exterior repels light rain and spray. Reinforced waterproof base layer on all lines. Optional Rain Cover module for complete downpour protection.

Hidden Anti-Theft Pocket

A flat, hidden pocket sits flush against your back panel — invisible from outside. RFID-resistant lining as standard. Ideal for passport, cards, and high-value items in transit.

Ergonomic Airflow Back Panel

Channelled high-density foam with a moisture-wicking mesh overlay. Designed for 8-hour carry comfort. Adjustable sternum strap with safety whistle included on all three lines.

YKK Zippers Throughout

Every single zipper across the pack and all modules is a genuine YKK — the global standard used by Arc'teryx, Peak Design, and Patagonia. Built to open and close millions of times.

Care Instructions

  • 01. Wipe exterior with a damp cloth and mild soap. Do not submerge or machine wash.
  • 02. Air dry fully before storage. Avoid direct heat sources or tumble drying.
  • 03. Zippers can be lightly lubricated with beeswax if sticking occurs.
  • 04. Reapply DWR spray (Nikwax or similar) annually or after heavy use to maintain water resistance.
  • 05. Store uncompressed and away from direct sunlight to extend material life.
