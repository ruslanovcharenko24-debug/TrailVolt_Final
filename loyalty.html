Program Questions

Points are valid for 24 months from the date they are earned. If your account has no activity for 24 months, any remaining points will expire. We'll email you 30 days before any points are due to expire so you can take action.
Points unlock tier-based perks automatically as you earn them. Each tier comes with increasing discount codes and benefits. At Summit tier, your discount code TRIBE25 gives you 25% off every order. Points function as progress trackers — the discounts and perks are the reward.
Every purchase, review, and referral triggers an automatic email showing your updated points balance and current tier. You can also email hello@trailvolt.com at any time to request a points balance summary. A dedicated member dashboard is on our roadmap for 2026.
If you return a product within the 60-day return window, the points earned from that purchase will be deducted from your balance. If a deduction causes your balance to drop below a tier threshold, your tier will be adjusted accordingly. Referral and review points are not affected by returns.
